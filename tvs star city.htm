Launched: TVS Star City Plus Golden Edition 2015 at Rs. 48,934

Facebook
Twitter
Pinterest
WhatsApp

    TVS Motor Company today launched the ‘Special Gold Edition 2015 TVS Star City Plus’ motorcycle in the Indian two-wheeler market. The Special Gold Edition TVS Star City+ is priced at Rs. 48,934/- ex-Delhi and is currently available at all TVS Showrooms across the country. The launch also marks the completion of one year of the highly popular Star City Plus. The vehicle gets several additional features that adds to its overall profile. The motorcycle also gets white and gold colour combination.

    Also Read- TVS-BMW Ko3 300cc bike and TVS Apache 200

    “We have launched the Special Gold Edition TVS Star City Plus to commemorate thecompletion of one year of the extremely successful TVS StaR City+. This variant builds on the superiority of TVS StaR City+, with Gold theme styling along with exciting new features like tubeless tyres and USB charger. It endorses our commitment to introduce exciting products for our customers through continuous product innovations.We are confident that this Special Gold Edition TVS StaR City+ will also be a huge success with our customers.” said K N Radhakrishnan, President and CEO, TVS Motor Company.

    Read More- Upcoming TVS Bikes in India

    Talking about features, the Special Gold Edition 2015 TVS Star City Plus gets a white body colour with vibrant golden graphics, gold coloured mag wheels, gold coloured three dimensional brand logo, ‘Bike of the Year’ endorsement accompanying a gold coloured TVS logo on the visor, contrasting beige coloured side cover frame and a dual textured seat with red stitching, which is offset by red coloured shock absorbers. The special edition also gets high grip tubeless tyres and a USB mobile charging provision under the seat.

    Powering the special edition Star City Plus is the same 109.7cc motor that is capable of producing 8.30PS of power along with 8.70Nm of torque. Mated to a four-speed gearbox, the bike is claimed to return an impressive 86kmpl of mileage. The suspension mechanism comprises of telescopic front shock absorbers and five step adjustable at the rear.
